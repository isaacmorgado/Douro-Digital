B2B SaaS · Social Media Automation · Local Business Marketing

96% of small businesses use social media — but only to broadcast, not engage. Thousands of high-intent conversations happen daily (“best pizza in [city]”, “AC broke need help”, “dentist recommendations?”) and local businesses miss 99% of them. Viral-Kid needed a strategy to own this untapped category.

Existing tools only solve half the problem: Hootsuite and Buffer schedule YOUR posts. ManyChat replies to YOUR DMs. Nobody automates the process of finding viral, high-engagement conversations and inserting businesses into them. We created a new product category: proactive social engagement automation.

63% of consumers visit a business after a positive social interaction, yet most SMBs never engage beyond their own posts. Viral-Kid fills this gap — automatically finding viral conversations across Twitter, Reddit, YouTube, and Instagram, and joining them with authentic, contextual replies 24/7 for $199–$499/mo.
